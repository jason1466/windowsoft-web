Google Material Design
As of 2015 most of Google's mobile and desktop applications have applied the new design language including Gmail, YouTube, Google Drive, Google Docs, Google Maps, etc. It aspires to unite Google’s expansive product line under a rich set of design styles and principles.
  • A new design language developed by Google in 2014 that synthesizes traditional principles of design best practices with the innovation and possibility of technology and science.
  • Based on paper and ink, material design has physical surfaces and edges, seams and shadows that provide meaning about what you can touch.
  • Extends throughout Google's suite of web and mobile products, providing an integrated and consistent user experience across all platforms and applications.

WordPress is the most popular content management system on the Web. Started in 2003 as an open source blogging platform, it has evolved into a full content management system (CMS) through thousands of plugins, widgets and themes. WordPress is used by more than 27.5% of the top 10 million websites.
  • Using a web template system, you can switch between different themes, changing both the look and the functionality of your web site.

  • 10,000+ WordPress themes and 50,000+ plugins available that make SEO and social sharing a breeze
